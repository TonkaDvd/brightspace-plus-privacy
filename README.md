 Privacy policy for the Brightspace PLUS Chrome extension
Brightspace PLUS – Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
What data we collect
Brightspace PLUS reads the content of your USC Brightspace grades page solely to display and calculate your course grade. This includes assignment names, point values, and grade structures visible on the page.
How data is stored
All data (custom categories, grade scale settings, hypothetical scores) is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. It never leaves your device.
What we don't do
We do not collect personally identifiable information. We do not transmit any data to external servers. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
