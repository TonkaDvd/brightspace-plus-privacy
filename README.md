# Brightspace PLUS – Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

Brightspace PLUS reads the content of your USC Brightspace grades page solely to display and calculate your course grade. This includes assignment names, point values, and grade structures visible on the page.

All data you create — custom categories, grade scale settings, and hypothetical scores — is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`. It never leaves your device. We do not collect personally identifiable information, we do not transmit any data to external servers, and we do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Questions? Open an issue on this repository.
